##  About

**Myth or Hack?** is an interactive, fast-paced quiz game that challenges players to distinguish between hack and myth. Each round presents a scenario — your job is to judge: **real hack** or **total myth**?

> *"Your phone is listening to you." — Myth or Hack?*
> *"Hackers can unlock your car via a relay attack." — Myth or Hack?*

##  How It Works

```
1. Press start
2. Read the scenario presented on screen
3. Tap MYTH or HACK
4. See the verdict + a short explanation
5. Accumulate points for correct, fast answers
6. View your final score and share it
```
